Marbles

A game of marbles
Equipment: Some marbles!

Let the kids play a game of marbles

Give the kids a pile of marbles each - the aim is to win all your opponents marbles

Take turns to try and hit one of your opponents marbles with one of yours - if you manage it you win that marble! You keep going until you miss and then it's your opponents turn
