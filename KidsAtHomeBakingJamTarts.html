Jam Tarts

Making jam tarts
Ingredients: ready-made shortcrust pastry (or make some pastry yourself), jam

Let your children make some jam tarts

Preheat the oven to 180 deg C then sprinkle some flour on a table or other flat surface. Give the kids a square of pasrty each and let them roll it out using a rolling pin

Give the children a round pastry cutter and let them cut out circles from the pastry (use a cup f you don't have a pasrty cutter)

Put the pasrty circles in a cupcake tray

Add about 1 teaspoon of jam to each pastry circle (not too much or it will leak everywhere)

Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes until the pastry is golden

Allow to cool before eating as the jam will be very hot!
