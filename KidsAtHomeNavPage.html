Welcome to Kids@Home - a website with activity ideas for kids of all ages ! The aim of this website is to give you some ideas for fun things you can do with your children which don't cost much or require lots of specialist equipment - just pens, crayons, paper, glue and other items you probably have hanging around the house. Some of the ideas are quick and some take a bit longer. You can modify them depending on the ages of your children, use them as starting point for bigger projects or just have a bit of fun - it's up to you. There's no right or wrong way to do the activities and if you or your kids think of a more fun way to play with the ideas then just go for it !
