Sardines

A reverse game of hide-and-seek

For this game one person hides and everyone else counts

When the time is up everyone goes looking for the person who is hiding

If you find the "hider" you keep quiet and hide with them in their hiding place

As more and more players find the hider everyone is squashed into the same hiding place like sardines!

The last person to find everyone is the hider in the next game !
