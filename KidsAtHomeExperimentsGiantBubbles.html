Giant Bubbles

How to make some giant bubbles !!
Equipment: washing-up liquid, water, straws, plastic tray or plastic covered table

You need a wipe-clean, flat surface for this activity, a table is perfect if it's smooth, flat and waterproof ! But you could use a plastic or metal tray if you like.

Mix 1 - 2 teaspoons of washing-up liquid with 2 - 4 teaspoons of water

Mix it up to get a very slightly bubbly mixture (you can do this straight on the table)

Use the straws to gently blow the tiny bubbles up in to really big ones!

You can join the big bubbles up to make huge caterpillars or blow bubbles inside bubbles!
