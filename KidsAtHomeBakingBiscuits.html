Oat Biscuits

Making oat biscuits
Ingredients: 75g demerera sugar, 75g butter, 75g self raising flour, 75g porridge oats, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1 tablespoon golden syrup, 1 tablespoon milk

An easy oat biscuit recipe with no rolling out

Preheat the oven to 170 deg C and line a baking tray with baking parchment or greasproof paper

Let the children weigh out the oats, flour, sugar and baking powder and mix them together in a large bowl

Using a saucepan or a microwave melt the butter, milk and syrup

Add the melted butter mixture to the dry ingredients and let the kids mix it all together

Let the kids spoon out about 12 dollops of mixture onto the baking tray and make sure there's some space between them as the biscuits will spread when they're cooking

Bake in the centre of the oven for 10 minutes or until the biscuits are golden brown

Leave the biscuits to cool for 5 minutes on the tray before moving them
