Toys Birthday Party

How to make a toys birthday party !!
Equipment: paper, scissors, sticky tape, pens/pencils/crayons and of course some toys !! (Optional: cake cases and tissues/kitchen roll)

If the kids are a bit bored why not have a toys birthday party? Get each child to choose a toy whose birthday it is - remember to bring more toys as party guests!

Fold some paper in half to make birthday cards then let the kids decorate them

Cut A4 paper in half in a zig zag shape and sitck the ends together with sticky tape to make crowns (for the toys or the kids!)

Cut circles of paper to be plates and let the kids draw party food on them. Or you could let the kids draw pictures of food on more paper then cut out the food to place on the plates

If you have any cake cases you can scrunch up kitchen roll or tissues (stick it at the bottom to stop it unravelling) place it in the cases and let the kids draw icing on with felt tips!

Sit the toys on the floor with their crowns, cards and party food and enjoy the party!

Maybe play some music so the toys can play musical statues? (They should be good at that!) Remember it doesn't matter what any of these things look like - it's just a game!
